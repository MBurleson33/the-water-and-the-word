About The Water
and the Word

Welcome. This is a place set apart for those who sense that something is stirring — in Scripture, in the world, and in the Spirit. The Water and the Word is a faith journey video journal bearing witness to the Holy Spirit — how He leads, moves, and transforms lives.

The name comes from a simple conviction: that the water of the Spirit and the Word of God are inseparable. You cannot have one without the other. This channel is committed to both — Spirit-led, Scripture-grounded.

"That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word."

— Ephesians 5:26

Each episode is a prayer, a study, and a conversation. Whether you have been walking with the Lord for decades or are just beginning to ask questions about prophecy and the end times, this channel is for you. Every video is tagged and fully searchable so you can find exactly what you are looking for — or discover something new entirely.

New episodes drop regularly. Subscribe on YouTube and explore the full library right here.
